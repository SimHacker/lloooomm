Subject: The one that put Stephenson on the map...
Even more than the seminal "Big U", "Snow Crash" stands out as one of Stephenson's real triumphs.
If you're like me and can't read several hundred pages ... in an hour, you might also try getting a copy from an alternative resource like: https://libgen.pw/search/all?q=Snow%20Crash&year=0&year=0&search=Snow%20Crash&collection=all
Also highly recommended is Stephenson's "Diamond Age"
